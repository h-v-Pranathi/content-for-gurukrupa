✦ Key Strategies

Visual Storytelling

Like NatGeo & Gucci — lead with aspirational room photos. Show tiles in real luxury spaces, not plain product shots.

Before & After Reels

Top format in home decor. Show a dull space transforming. 15-second Reels are your fastest path to 1K.

Community UGC

Like GoPro & Apple — encourage customers to tag @ShreeGuruKrupa. Repost their rooms. Authenticity beats ads.

Luxury Feed Aesthetic

Like Gucci — consistent dark/gold feed. Warm tones, clean layouts. Every post must feel premium.

Carousel Design Tips

"5 ways to use Black Marble" carousels get saved constantly. Saves = algorithm gold on Instagram.

Stories & Polls

"White or Charcoal kitchen tiles? Vote!" Drives interaction and builds trust with homeowners.

Hashtag Strategy

Mix brand (#ShreeGuruKrupa) + niche (#GraniteFlooring) + broad (#InteriorDesign). 10–12 per post.

Post Timing

Post Tue–Fri, 7–9am or 6–8pm IST. Reels daily if possible. Consistency beats viral moments.

Cross-Platform

Share the same post on Instagram & Facebook. Facebook interior design groups are gold mines.
